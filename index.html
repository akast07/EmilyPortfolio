My name is Emily Antinozzi and I am an artist based outside Dallas, Texas. I am a graduate of the University of North Texas, where I studied studio art and anthropology, and I have completed a six week summer program in Illustration at the Rhode Island School of Design. My pictures are primarily done in a mixture of colored pencils, gouache and watercolors, but I also enjoy collage and photography. I am most inspired by and aspiring to work in children's illustration.
